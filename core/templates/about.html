About Community Files

A platform built by creators, for creators

Our Mission

Community Files is dedicated to creating a safe, reliable, and user-friendly platform where creators can share their work with the world. We believe in empowering independent developers, artists, musicians, and content creators by providing them with the tools they need to reach their audience.

What We Offer

Secure Sharing

All files are scanned and stored securely with verified uploads.

Community Driven

User ratings, reviews, and feedback help maintain quality.

Fast Downloads

Optimized servers ensure quick and reliable file downloads.

Mobile Friendly

Access your files anywhere with our responsive design.

Community Guidelines

To maintain a safe and respectful environment, we require all members to:

  • Only upload files you have the right to share
  • Provide accurate descriptions and categorization
  • Respect intellectual property rights
  • Report any inappropriate content
  • Be respectful in reviews and comments

Built With

  • Django Framework
  • SQLite Database
  • Email Verification
  • Bootstrap CSS
  • Secure Authentication
  • Responsive Design
